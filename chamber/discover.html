Demographics

In 2022, Vista, CA had a population of 98.4k, the 5 largest ethnic groups in Vista, CA are White (Non-Hispanic) (37.4%), White (Hispanic) (19.7%), Two+ (Hispanic) (19%), Other (Hispanic) (9.06%), and Asian (Non-Hispanic) (4.78%)
